Judging Criteria

Each judge will be grading the hacks on the following scale.

  • Completeness - 1 to 5 points
    • Does it seem to be bug-free, or were there obvious problems during the presentation?
    • Did the team show you everything their app could do, or were they selling you vaporware?
  • Technical Difficulty - 1 to 5 points
    • Was there a particular challenge the team had to overcome?
    • Did you know this was even possible?
    • Were you surprised they would attempt something so ambitious, or was this something any highschooler could do on a weekend?
  • Originality - 1 to 5
    • Does this hack break new ground, or is it simply parroting an existing product?
    • Did you learn something new by watching the presentation?
    • If itâ€™s based on an existing product, does it improve its predecessor in some meaningful way?
  • Pure Awesomeness - 1 to 15 points
    • Is it beautiful?
    • Did your jaw drop?
    • Did you laugh?
    • Do you wish this were your idea?
    • Was it your favorite?
    • Do you think this team should win?
